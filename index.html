About IGIDR

Our Mission

IGIDR is an advanced research institute established and fully funded by the Reserve Bank of India for carrying out research on development issues from a multi-disciplinary perspective.

Location

IGIDR is located in Mumbai, India's financial capital, providing a unique environment for studying economic and financial issues.

Academic Excellence

The institute offers M.Sc., Ph.D. and M.Phil. programs in economics and has a strong research focus on development studies.

About MFC

The Macroeconomics and Finance Conference is an annual event organized by IGIDR, bringing together leading economists, researchers, and policymakers to discuss contemporary issues in macroeconomics and finance.
